## Research Project — Reddit Marketing for B2B SaaS

### Why this topic
Reddit is one of the most underutilized channels in B2B SaaS marketing.
With AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity increasingly citing Reddit threads,
and Google giving Reddit content massive visibility boosts post-2024,
the opportunity for B2B brands is significant and growing fast.

### Repository Structure

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `/research/sources.md` | 10 experts with links, dates, and annotations |
| `/research/linkedin-posts/` | Posts organized by author |
| `/research/youtube-transcripts/` | Transcripts organized by video |
| `/research/other/` | Case studies and additional materials |

### How I chose these experts
I prioritized practitioners over commentators — founders who grew SaaS
products via Reddit, consultants with documented client results, and
specialists who built services around Reddit marketing.
Generic "top 10 Reddit tips" bloggers were excluded.
