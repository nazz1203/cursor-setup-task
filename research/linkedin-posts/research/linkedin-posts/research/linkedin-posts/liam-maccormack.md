**Date:** 17/05/26
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/liammaccormackgrowth_most-early-stage-b2b-saas-companies-measuring-share-7462155012086059008-nzSh/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAEB2ORgB8BhOFa3PsYwQ3WQxOm1LuXzOV8o

Most early-stage B2B SaaS companies measuring their AEO program are tracking vanity metrics. And tbh, most of the ones measuring their SEO program are too.

"ChatGPT is recommending us as a solution!" Cool. Any demo's from that? Trials? Pipeline?

Meanwhile: your paid campaigns have no conversion tracking. Your website says nothing specific about what you actually do. And when someone submits your demo form, it takes 3 days for follow-up to happen. 

But you're spending $1000's/mo watching your LLM's share of voice steadily improve!

AEO and SEO both have a place. But if you can't tie them to pipeline, you're celebrating visibility, not results. If you're Seed or Series A and you're trying to figure out your growth engine, AEO/SEO should not be your priority.

Fix your fundamentals first. Test paid channels. Not because they always work, but because you get signal in weeks instead of months. Nail your messaging. Build your conversion infrastructure so when someone raises their hand, you don't lose them.

Then play the long game.

But stop obsessing over AI citations when you can't measure what they do. 

I promise you: AEO isn't going to save your pipeline this quarter.

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/liammaccormackgrowth_mining-reddit-for-customer-insights-community-activity-7151226264157065217-KV2-?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAEB2ORgB8BhOFa3PsYwQ3WQxOm1LuXzOV8o

If you're not mining Reddit for customer insights & community, well, you should be. Here's my setup & strategy with my clients 👇 :

It's honestly straightforward. 

1. Identify subreddits 🎯 

2. List your keywords 📄 

3. Setup Zapier to search those subreddits for those keywords for you 🔍 

4. Get Zapier to send a Slack message when it finds what you're looking for 💬 

5. Build your community engagement/customer insights strategy around those findings 💻 

6. Go be a helpful part of your community (and yes - EMPHASIS on "helpful") 💥 

Implement this automation and I promise, the insights you're going to get and the community you can build have BIG direct business impact.
