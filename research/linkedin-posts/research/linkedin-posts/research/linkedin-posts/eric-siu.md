Running a business is starting to feel like a video game.

I'm not kidding.

I asked one agent how our Meta ads were doing. It pulled the live numbers in seconds.

Then I told another: study what's already winning, generate 500 new variations, and kill everything that isn't working.

Done. All inside a Slack thread.

Oracle pulls the data.
Picasso builds the creative.

I just call the shots.

And every day there's a new skill to install. A new command. A new agent.

That's why it feels like a game.

You're constantly leveling up your setup.
